# about us

Quality Hyigen is our pirority we never compromise on quality.

we were pioneers of cold-pressed juicing in Lebanon, and were (and still are!) the only company to offer a fully certified organic juice cleanse. We listened to our customers and grew organically, making the natural transition from juicing as therapy, to supporting a more balanced and healthy lifestyle.

Today the Sherk Juice is about more than juice and smoothies. It’s a place that takes a holistic approach to beauty, wellness and longevity. We focus on local, nutrient dense foods and a program of workshops that supports our quest and right to live better.
